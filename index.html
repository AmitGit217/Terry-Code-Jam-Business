Volunteer responsibilities:

  1. poo pickup -kangaroo stomachs are very sensitive so we must keep the grass they graze clean.
  2. cleaning joey house.
  3. bottle feeding joeys
  4. preparing snack baskets for joeys - growing joeys need three types of dirt, bark, hey, and grain in order to build mussle and get all the nutrians and minerals they need!
  5. joey walking - exercise is important for developing legs!
